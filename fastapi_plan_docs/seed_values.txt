## Notes
- **flight_number** format: `AX-XXXX` — change the `AX` prefix to match your airline code.
- **gate** format: Letter (terminal) + Number (gate). 6 terminals (A–F), 4 gates each = 24 unique gates.
- These values map to the `time_slot` table via `time_slot_id` (0–23).
- When seeding the `flight` table, you will also need a `departure_date` and an `aircraft_id` (from your `aircraft` seed).
